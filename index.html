Drum Star

Hit the falling gems right on the beat and become a drum star!

👆 Tap “Sound check” first — browsers keep sound off until you tap!

For grown-ups: Lessons unlock one at a time, just like Melodics. Earn at least ★ to open the next one. Play by tapping the pads or with keyboard keys D F J K L (left hand on D–F, right hand on J–K–L). Practice mode slows every lesson down — great for the first try. Progress is saved automatically. Each drummer has their own name, avatar and stars — tap Switch at the top to change players or add a sibling.
No sound? Tap “Sound check” once (browsers block audio until the first tap) and turn the volume up. Drum Star now plays even with the iPad’s side mute switch on; on much older iPads, flip that switch off too.
iPad tip: in Safari tap Share → Add to Home Screen to install Drum Star as a full-screen app.
