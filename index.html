Drum Star

Hit the falling gems right on the beat and become a drum star!

👆 Tap “Sound check” first — browsers keep sound off until you tap!

For grown-ups: Lessons unlock one at a time, just like Melodics. Earn at least ★ to open the next one. Play by tapping the pads or with keyboard keys D F J K L (left hand on D–F, right hand on J–K–L). Practice mode slows every lesson down — great for the first try. Progress is saved automatically.
No sound? Tap “Sound check” once (browsers block audio until the first tap). On an iPhone or iPad, also flip the side mute switch off and turn the volume up — Web Audio follows the ringer switch.
iPad tip: in Safari tap Share → Add to Home Screen to install Drum Star as a full-screen app.
